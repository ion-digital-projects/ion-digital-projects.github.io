The Story of Soy

How much do you know about this ubiquitous bean? Take our quiz to find out.

By Morgan McFall-Johnsen  |  Published on May 16, 2016

Soy is in almost everything we eat - whether it’s an animal that was fed soy all of its life, a health bar with ambiguously-named ingredients, or the special milk you request in your Starbucks coffee every morning. This simple bean has become ubiquitous in our lives and highly controversial. So let’s debunk some myths and get our facts straight.
